# Companion App

> Paste a confusing dating chat, get an honest read of what their behavior is actually saying — not a guess at their feelings.

Companion App helps you cut through the ambiguity of modern dating conversations. Instead of guessing at hidden feelings or projecting hope onto mixed signals, it focuses on what someone's **behavior** actually communicates: their effort, consistency, and intent.

## Why

When a chat feels confusing, the instinct is to ask "what do they mean by this?" — a question that invites wishful interpretation. Companion App reframes it to "what is this behavior telling me?" Behavior is observable. Patterns are honest. The goal is clarity, not comfort.

## What it does

- **Reads the conversation, not your hopes.** Paste a snippet of a chat and get an analysis grounded in what was said and done.
- **Surfaces patterns.** Highlights consistency (or inconsistency) in effort, responsiveness, and follow-through.
- **Gives an honest read.** Tells you what the behavior suggests, even when it isn't what you want to hear.
- **Avoids mind-reading.** It interprets actions, not unspoken emotions.

## How it works

1. Paste a confusing or ambiguous dating chat.
2. The app analyzes the exchange — tone, effort, reciprocity, and signals.
3. You get a straightforward read on what the behavior actually indicates.
